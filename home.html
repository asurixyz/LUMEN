LUMEN Dashboard

illumination through data

This dashboard provides access to a series of datasets visualizing the primary forces shaping the 21st-century global landscape. The data is organized into three principal sections: global dynamics, regional economic data, and an analysis of strategic maritime corridors.

I. Global & National Dynamics

  • Interactive Global Trade Map →

    A map-based visualization of global trade partnerships with the US and China from 2000-2022, showing the shift in economic influence over time.

  • Geopolitical & Economic Futures →

    An interactive dashboard of long-term projections covering GDP, population, R&D spending, military expenditure, and other key national indicators.

II. Contemporary Regional Data

  • Asian Economic Indicators →

    A focused look at key economic indicators for major Asian economies and the ASEAN bloc.

III. Strategic Maritime Corridors

  • South China Sea →

    Data on trade, investments, and energy dependence in a critical region of strategic competition.

  • Bay of Bengal →

    Analysis of regional trade and investment trends in an emerging global supply chain corridor.

  • East African Coast →

    Insights into the growing trade and investment partnerships between major Asian economies and East Africa.

  • Persian Gulf →

    Data on trade and financial flows in a region vital for global energy security and economic stability.
