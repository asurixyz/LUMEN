Maritime Trade & Investment Dashboards

Explore interactive visualizations built from on-repo CSV datasets.

About the data. Each dashboard reads CSV files in this repository (no database, no server). The series typically include annual trade volumes (in USD) and investment flows (e.g., FDI, infrastructure, security, development assistance). Countries and categories are plotted as separate traces so you can compare levels and rates of change over time.

How to read the charts. Most pages include multiple line charts: one for total trade and one or more for investment breakdowns. Hover to see exact values; use the legend to toggle series. If you’re on mobile, rotate to landscape for more detail.

What to look for.

  • South China Sea: Rising trade volumes across major economies; watch energy-dependence shares and the composition of Chinese investment (oil & gas, military infrastructure, ports/BRI).
  • Bay of Bengal: Gradual growth in regional trade; compare investment series to see which economies lead during different periods.
  • East African Coast: Expanding trade links; investment lines can highlight sectoral focus and timing of major projects.
  • Persian Gulf: High absolute trade levels; key players’ trade compares large economies’ exposure to the Gulf over time.

Notes. Figures may be rounded; some CSVs aggregate categories. Column names drive the legends—if you change headers, the charts will update accordingly.
