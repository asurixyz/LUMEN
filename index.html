A Data-Driven Analysis of Geopolitical & Economic Futures

By Venkatakrishnan Asuri

This project presents a collection of interactive data visualizations examining the intersection of economic development, demographic shifts, and strategic competition. The analysis is structured into several key areas: an interactive map of global trade partnerships with the US and China, long-term projections of national indicators through 2075, and a focused examination of investment flows within critical maritime corridors.
